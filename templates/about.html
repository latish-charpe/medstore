What is MedStore?

Our Purpose

The goal of MedStore is to make healthcare management efficient and accessible. Whether you are searching for specific medicines, looking for wellness products, or managing store inventory, MedStore offers a user-friendly interface to handle it all with ease.

Who is it For?

  • For Customers: A reliable platform to check medicine availability, browse categories, and get health tips.
  • For Store Managers: A robust dashboard to manage inventory, update stock, and track medicines efficiently.

This application is designed to simulate a real-world pharmacy management experience.
